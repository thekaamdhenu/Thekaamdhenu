Strongly recommending and boosting Indian breed

Indian breeds are considered as Pure Yielders and indeed crossbreeded for better yield of dairy products and for better offspring. These offsprings are later strong as compared to Parent Breed

  • Treating cow as a member of a family
  • Selecting Cows such as Sahiwal and Rathi for Greater Production
  • Crossbreeding Cows with Tharparkar breed, Krishna Valley Breed that are strong in both recessiveness and appearance
  • Maintaining fixed time to farmers for yielding milk.
  • Maintaining a Together-with Environment for both Cow and its calves.

Treating Cow as a member, not an animal

Cows have a great connection with Ancient Spiritual India It is Indeed a faith that Lord Krishna had a lovely place for Cows. No doubt we Indians are trying to boost that same notion in all Indian, irrespective of what community a person resides.

  • Regular checkup of Cows, calves and Bull together for Healthy Yield
  • Rich Fodder provided to cattles.
  • Dung and Urine Treated in farms for production of Nutrient Rich Manure and Disinfectant purposes.
  • Selected cattle holders with same notion of care with cattles.

Not only working, but having fun too

  • Connecting Different Herders of India For A Noble Cause.
