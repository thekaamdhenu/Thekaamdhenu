Indian Bulls and Cattles are strongly being recommended for New India

  • Fardeen Ahmed|
  • 10|
  • 4|
  • comments

Usually Greek bulls are generally used for crossbreeding to yield recessive gene offspring. A new initiative is on a thrust, encouraging Indian Breeds for Crossbreeding and Boosting Milk and Dairy Products. There is no doubt a greater yield by Greek crossbreed, but is it pure to that extent? Here is where Indian bulls are supported. The purity factor differentiates and supports Indian Breeds over Greek Bulls. The Milk produced by Indian bulls are much nutrient containing. The Alpha-2 Milk produced are rich in iron minerals and is perfect for balance diet. Regarding higher yield generated, no doubt foreign crosbreed give more milk, but lacks rich nutrients compared to Indian breeds. Firmly believeing facts and figure It is very much clear that produced milk is better in all condition as compare to any other milk. Other boosting factors should be contributing in this noble cause. There is a need of people of and contributing hands. We need people in this sector, because no sector is small or big, only contribution matters. if one contributes, he or she will no doubt have a fruitful life.
