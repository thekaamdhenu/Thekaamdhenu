The Kamdhenu

What we do?

Thinking and Implementation are two different things.

We strive for two things, "Spirituality and Purity". So, we do our best by connecting people, both consumer and producer with same notion, by same feeling of regarding Cow as a family member.

Boosting Spirituality and Purity

Our Initiative is to raise a notion of boosting The same Spiritual Connection with Cow as we had in our Pure Ancient India. Regarding Cow not as a milk yielder, but as a member of family.

Connecting People

Mass Indian Dairy Producers as well as Consumers will be able to connect people with exchange of Dairy Goods and Services.

Increase in Indian Goods Productivity

Use of Indian product not only will give boost to Herders and Consumers, but to Indian Economy as well.

Expected Vision : Rich Milk India

Our Vision is to boost India by boosting Milk sectors, which are being progressively degraded,manipulated and Dominated by foreign countries and producers.
